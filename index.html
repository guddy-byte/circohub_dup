About CIRCO Hub Nigeria

CIRCO Hub Nigeria focuses on promoting a circular economy business model and product design through the proven CIRCO methodology.

We offer training, workshops, and tailored support with the aim to help your company rethink product design, extend product life cycles, and create sustainable business models. We help both established companies and startups to embrace sustainability, reduce environmental impact, and enhance their competitive edge.

CIRCO Hub Nigeria is the first CIRCO Hub in Africa, an initiative focused on supporting businesses to innovate and reimagine their products, services, and operations using circular economy principles. It aims to help businesses transition from a traditional linear economic model (take, make, dispose) to a sustainable, circular model that reduces waste and maximizes resource efficiency.

Any business that is interested in embedding circularity into its operations, whether small, medium, or large enterprises, is eligible to participate in CIRCO Hub Nigeria. The initiative is designed to cater to various sectors, including manufacturing, fashion, construction, and technology, among others.

The schedule for the first program is January 2025. You can also stay updated by following our official communication channels on social media or by subscribing to our newsletter on our website.
